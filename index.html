welcome to the school website project

The School was completed in January 1918, for the purpose of educating Tucson's african-American students. The school was named afterPaul Laurence Dunbar, a renowed African-American poet.African-American children in first through ninth grades atttened until 1951, when de jure segregation was eliminated from the school systems of Arizona. When segregation in Arizona was eliminated, School became the non-segregated John Spring Junior High School, and continued as such until 1978 when the school was closed permanently
